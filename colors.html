Color

Color Theory is a tricky thing. It's hard to totally understand so when developing your pallate, take some time to do your research and make sure there's harmony in your choices.

Primary Colors

These are usually used as the main color on the site. Things like links, branding, etc. The main color is $colPrimary-03. This is so we can go lighter and darker by two shades. You can change this to function however you like, just keep in mind the components are setup to mainly use this setup. As always, you can just go in and change them.
