Spacing

These are the units used throughout the site. You can easily adjust the base and all the other units will scale.

Spacing Scale

I generally like to use 4 or 8 as my base unit. It makes the math easy and keeps thing feeling uniform.

Below is the spacing setup in this system, feel free to change it if you like.
